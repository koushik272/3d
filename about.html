About Keshav Memorial Institute of Technology

Vision

KMIT aspires to be the fountainhead in producing highly skilled, globally competent engineers, striving to make India a world leader in software products and services. The institute is committed to achieving global excellence in teaching, research, consulting, and placements by fostering a conducive environment in the fields of computer sciences and information technology with a professional and global outlook. KMIT emphasizes nurturing core values that drive continuous advancement and learner autonomy.

Mission

  • Create a faculty pool with a deep understanding and passion for algorithmic thought processes.
  • Impart skills beyond the university-prescribed curriculum to transform students into well-rounded computer science professionals.
  • Inculcate an ability in students to pursue lifelong learning in information technology using multimodal platforms, including e-learning and blended learning.
  • Encourage collaboration with reputed organizations for research, consultancy, and industrial interactions to provide real-world exposure.
  • Foster an entrepreneurial mindset through the Entrepreneur Development Cell, exposing students to diverse domains and modern business environments.

Outcomes

KMIT delivers exceptional educational outcomes, preparing students for success in the tech industry:

  • Industry-Ready Graduates: Through initiatives like Trishul, Finishing School, UTTKARSH, TESSELLATOR, and SONET, students gain advanced technical skills and problem-solving abilities.
  • Project-Driven Learning: Focus on real-time projects in domains like Biomedical Informatics, Agri-Informatics, Defense Technology, and GSoC, ensuring practical experience.
  • Global Recognition: Students have excelled in events like the Smart India Hackathon 2024, with teams like Canon Crew and BinaryBrains0101 reaching the finals.
  • Holistic Development: Programs foster professional and ethical responsibilities, preparing students for global careers.
